Signs You May Need Treatment
  • Cravings, loss of control, or repeated failed attempts to quit
  • Neglecting school/work/family responsibilities
  • Withdrawal symptoms or needing more to get the same effect
  • Risky behavior, legal issues, or financial problems
  • Mental health changes: anxiety, depression, mood swings
üìû
First Steps
  • Talk to a trusted adult, doctor, or counselor
  • Call a verified helpline for confidential guidance
  • Get a professional assessment to choose the right level of care
  • Involve supportive family or friends when safe
Remember: Asking for help is a sign of strength, not weakness. Early treatment improves outcomes.
